There are three instruction formats in the MIPS architecture.
1) R-Format: Instructions that only use REGISTERS
2) I-Format: Instructions that use IMMEDIATE values 
3) J-Format: Instructions that contain ADDRESSES

Supported Registers: $t0-$t9, $s0-$s7

Supported Instructions: 
R-Format: add, addu, sub, subu, and, or, nor, slt, sltu, sll, srl, jr 
I-Format: beq, bne, addi, addiu, andi, ori, slti, sltiu, lui, lw, sw
J-Format: j, jal
